Okukinu Onsen

Very remote onsen near Nikko

Okukinu Onsen (奥鬼怒温泉) is a collection of four remote hot spring ryokan in the far back of a remote valley in the mountains of Tochigi Prefecture. The ryokan offer some of the most atmospheric, rustic outdoor baths in the Kanto Region, worlds apart from bustling Tokyo and built up Kinugawa Onsen.

Hakone Hot Springs

Highly popular onsen destination near Tokyo

Hakone has been one of Japan's most popular hot spring resorts for centuries. Nowadays, more than a dozen springs provide hot spring water to the many bath houses and ryokan in the Hakone region.

Kusatsu Hot Spring Baths

Pretty town with celebrated onsen water

Kusatsu has the largest natural flow of hot spring water in all of Japan, including the waters that surfaces at the Yubatake in the town center. The sulfurous, highly acidic waters have been considered among Japan's best for centuries, and the resort town has constantly occupied a top spot in onsen rankings. It is difficult to beat Kusatsu in terms of quality and quantity of its waters.
